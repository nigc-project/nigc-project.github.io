Video: How a Native American tribe fought government over gambling and won

Or, read the article here.

This video is the story of the Fort McDowell Yavapai Nation and their struggle with setting up slot machines after the passage of the Indian Gaming Regulatory Act. They had a fight with the FBI after trucks came to take away the slot machines, which the government asserted were being operated illegally. The tribe resisted and advocated for their sovereignty, setting up physical blockades to prevent the trucks from leaving and marching on Arizona's capitol.
